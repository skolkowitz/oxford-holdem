How to Play

Build the best word using your Hand and the Board.

  • Draft: Pick the best 3 cards from 5 options.
  • The Flop: 3 community cards are dealt.
  • Swap: Discard up to 2 cards to draw new ones.
  • The River: The final card. 2x SCORE for the letter in this slot!
