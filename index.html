How to Play

Build the best word using your Hole Cards and the Board.

  • Draft: Pick 1, 2, or 3 hole cards. Fewer cards = higher multipliers!
  • The Flop: 3 community cards are dealt. Discard up to 2 cards to draw new ones.
  • The Turn: The fourth community card is dealt. Discard up to 2 cards to draw new ones.
  • The River: The final card. 2x SCORE for the letter in this slot!
